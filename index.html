Walter White Crystal Selector

Instructions

Jesse will give you a random number at the start of the game

By clicking a crystal you will add points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to Jesse's number, you lose if your score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click

Every time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
